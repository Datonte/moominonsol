The Long Winter

In the Moomin winter story, everything slows down. The valley goes quiet, friends disappear, and even the strongest characters feel lost until spring finally returns.

That’s exactly where the Solana trenches are today. The market is cold, people are tired, bundlers and rugs have pushed traders away, and retail has basically gone into hibernation.

The Return of Spring

The irony is, we’re literally in winter right now. But just like in Moominvalley, winter doesn’t last forever. Spring always comes back, and when it does, the valley comes alive again.

Moomin on Solana is built around that same belief: we may be in the coldest part of the cycle, but this is where the real bounce-back begins.

If you’ve survived this far, you’re here for the spring.
